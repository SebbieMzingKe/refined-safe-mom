About Us

Welcome to our Health Prediction App! This platform is designed to assist healthcare professionals and patients by predicting health conditions based on various physiological parameters. Our mission is to leverage the power of machine learning to provide early warning signs for potential health risks.

Our Mission

Our goal is to create a tool that helps improve health outcomes by allowing users to input key metrics and receive instant predictions. By doing so, we aim to support both preventive care and ongoing health management.

Features

  • Secure User Registration and Login
  • Health Prediction Based on Multiple Parameters
  • Support for Custom Models and AI-based Predictions
  • User Data Stored Securely

How It Works

By entering information like age, weight, blood pressure, and other medical data, our model provides insights into potential health risks. Our AI model has been trained on a large dataset and continuously improves as more data is entered. This ensures accurate and up-to-date predictions.
